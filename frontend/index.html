About Our Project

Revolutionizing Mental Healthcare in Pakistan

Nafsiyat AI is a pioneering mental wellness platform designed specifically for the Pakistani context. Recognizing the critical gap in accessible mental health services, our project aims to bridge the divide between those seeking help and professional support.

With a shortage of mental health professionals and widespread stigma surrounding mental health in Pakistan, Nafsiyat AI provides an anonymous, accessible, and culturally aware first line of support. Our AI-powered chatbot offers immediate assistance, while our teleconsultation feature connects users with licensed psychologists for professional care.

The platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with evidence-based Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles, making mental health support available 24/7 to anyone with an internet connection.

  • Culturally aware responses in Urdu and English
  • Privacy-first anonymous support system
  • Integration with professional mental health services
  • Community-driven peer support network
